Brief

Holidays are always great fun. Until you have spent the better part two hours trying to find a certain historical landmark that you just have to see in person. Thats where walk comes in. Walk is essential the tour guide in your pocket you never knew you needed. it essentially works as a facebook for landmarks, restaurants, bars and other niche locations.

skills Used

  • UX Research
  • UI Design
  • Prototyping

Research

Creating an App requires a-lot more than some flashy logo and layout. A lot of consideration has to go into the UX and UI. The meat and bones of the product. Keeping this in mind I found a few apps that were similar to mine and started to analyse them from the ground up. I wanted to get a complete understanding of what makes a great UX before going into this project. I kept a blog along the way which you can view here to see all the nitty gritty stuff.
